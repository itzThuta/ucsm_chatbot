New Canteen

Location: Located at right side of the road and west side of laboratory
Description:
Located opposite of the football field, the New Canteen at the University of Computer Studies, Mandalay, stands as a modern culinary hub catering to the diverse tastes and preferences of the university community. Constructed in 2021, this state-of-the-art facility offers a contemporary dining experience amidst a vibrant and welcoming ambiance.
With its spacious dining area and sleek interior design, the New Canteen provides students, faculty, and staff with a comfortable and inviting space to enjoy a variety of freshly prepared meals, snacks, and beverages. Whether indulging in traditional Burmese cuisine or savoring international delicacies, visitors to the New Canteen can expect quality food made from the finest ingredients.
Equipped with modern kitchen facilities and adhering to strict hygiene standards, the New Canteen prioritizes the health and well-being of its patrons, ensuring a safe and enjoyable dining experience for all. Additionally, its strategic location near the football field makes it a convenient stop for hungry spectators and athletes seeking refreshments during sporting events and recreational activities. There is only one way that can reach new canteen from main building.It is 760 meters long.

Old Canteen

Location: Located at left side of the road and west side of laboratory
Description:
Situated adjacent to the football field,the Old Canteen at the University of Computer Studies, Mandalay, has been a beloved institution since its establishment in 1997. Steeped in nostalgia and tradition, this iconic culinary landmark holds a special place in the hearts of generations of students, faculty, and alumni.
With its quaint charm and rustic ambiance, the Old Canteen exudes a sense of warmth and familiarity, reminiscent of simpler times. Here, patrons can relish in an array of timeless favorites and classic comfort foods, lovingly prepared with time-honored recipes passed down through the years.
Despite its age, the Old Canteen remains a bustling hub of activity, bustling with lively conversations and the aromatic scent of freshly cooked meals. From hearty breakfasts to quick lunches and afternoon snacks, the Old Canteen continues to serve as a cherished gathering spot where lifelong friendships are forged and cherished memories are made.While the New Canteen may boast modern amenities and sleek aesthetics, the Old Canteen holds a special place in the hearts of those who cherish its rich history and enduring legacy. As a testament to the university's heritage and commitment to community, the Old Canteen remains a beloved institution that continues to evoke feelings of nostalgia and camaraderie among all who pass through its doors. There is only one way that can reach new canteen from main building.It is 770 meters long.
